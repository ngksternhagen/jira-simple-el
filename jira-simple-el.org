* jira-rest.el

** An Emacs Lisp library for interfacing with JIRA's REST API, focusing on simplicity

** To Install & Run
    1. Place ``jira-simple.el`` somewhere on your load-path
    2. Add ``(require 'jira-simple)`` to your init.
